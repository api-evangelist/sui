The Sui Foundation announced its third Hydropower Fellowship cohort, offering six early-stage teams hands-on technical support, go-to-market guidance, and ecosystem introductions. Projects span agentic finance, real-world asset tokenization, and conversational UX, including Asseto, Audric, GEN, Kash, Predikt, and Transact.sh.
